Some of the most knowledgeable car owners we've worked with are doing their own restorations and simply need a professional opinion at a particular decision point. That's what the consultation service is for.

A consultation might cover paint system selection — which primer, which clear, which brand works best for a particular application. It might be a color development session where we develop a formula together and hand it over. It might be a critical review of the prep work before color goes on, to catch anything that would show up later.

We charge by the hour with no minimum. You get our full attention and honest opinion for the time you need it. For owners who are investing years of their own labour in a car, getting the paint specification right can be the difference between a result they're proud of and one they're not.
