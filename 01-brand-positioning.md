## 2. Ideal Customer Profile

**Primary customer:**
- Age: 45–70
- Owns one or more vintage vehicles valued at $30K+
- Has either restored cars before or is doing a first major restoration
- Values craftsmanship over speed
- Will travel for the right shop
- Likely to find you via: Instagram, car show referrals, marque-specific forums, Hagerty community

**Secondary customer:**
- Restoration shops outsourcing paint work
- Insurance restoration projects (high-value claims)
