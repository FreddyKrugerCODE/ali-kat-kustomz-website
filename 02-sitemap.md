# Sitemap & Information Architecture

```
/
├── /                          Home
├── /services/                 Services overview
│   ├── /services/full-restoration/
│   ├── /services/color-matching/
│   ├── /services/custom-color/

├── /portfolio/                Portfolio index (filterable by era/marque)
│   └── /portfolio/[slug]/     Individual project pages
├── /process/                  How we work — 7-step process
├── /about/                    Shop story, team, facility
├── /testimonials/             Reviews + video testimonials
├── /journal/                  Blog/news (optional, great for SEO)
│   └── /journal/[slug]/
├── /contact/                  Contact + quote request form
└── /thank-you/                Form submission confirmation
```

## Page Priorities (Build Order)

1. **Home** — first impression, must convert
2. **Portfolio** — proof of craft, drives most conversions
3. **Contact / Quote** — capture leads
4. **Services** — answers "what do you do"
5. **Process** — answers "how do you work"
6. **About** — answers "who are you"
7. **Testimonials** — social proof
8. **Journal** — SEO compounder, build later

## Conversion Funnel

```
Home/Portfolio (attract)
    ↓
Services/Process (educate)
    ↓
About/Testimonials (build trust)
    ↓
Contact/Quote (convert)
```

## Primary CTAs (Used Throughout Site)

- **Primary:** "Request a Quote" → /contact/
- **Secondary:** "See Our Work" → /portfolio/
- **Tertiary:** "Talk to a Specialist" → tel: link or /contact/
