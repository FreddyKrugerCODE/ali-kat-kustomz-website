## Section 3 — Our Process

**Headline:** Seven stages. No shortcuts.

**Intro:** Great paint isn't sprayed — it's prepared. Most of what we do happens before color ever touches the car.

1. **Intake & Documentation** — Photographs, color analysis, condition report
2. **Disassembly** — Trim, glass, hardware, lights — everything off, catalogued
3. **Strip to Bare Metal** — Media blast or chemical strip, depending on panel
4. **Bodywork & Block-Sanding** — Panel straightening, filler work, primer
5. **Sealer & Color** — Hand-mixed color, sprayed in our climate-controlled booth
6. **Clear & Cure** — Multiple clear coats, baked, then rested
7. **Color Sand & Polish** — Wet-sanded flat, machine-polished to final gloss
